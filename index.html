SurpriseNet

Yi-Wei Chen, Hung-Shin Lee, Yen-Hsing Chen, Hsin-Min Wang, "SurpriseNet: Melody Harmonization Conditioning on User-controlled Surpise Contours," submitting to Proc. ISMIR, 2021.

In this paper, we proposed SurpriseNet, which is based on a conditional VAE model and combines a surprise contour from the transition probability in a Markov chain, to achieve a user-controlled melody harmonization task. From the generated samples, we observed that the model could accurately generate various harmonic chord progressions according to the given surprise contours.

The results of experiments on the Hooktheory Lead Sheet Dataset, which contains rich intonation data, such as Roman, symbol, secondary, and mode data.
